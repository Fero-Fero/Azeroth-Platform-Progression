The Scarab Gong lies silent — for now. Across Azeroth, factions must unite to deliver bandages, bars, and beasts to the war effort. Only when the collective work is done will the gates of Ahn'Qiraj open.

War Effort

  • Realm-wide resource turn-ins in major cities
  • Silithus fortifications and event quests
  • Race to complete the gong ceremony and unlock raids

Stand Together

This is not a solo endeavor. Miners, herbalists, skinners, and farmers all have a role. Officers: coordinate schedules, track progress, and keep morale high — the insects do not wait politely.

When the gong sounds, the desert will answer.
