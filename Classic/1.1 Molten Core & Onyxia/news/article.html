Prepare to descend into the heart of Blackrock Mountain — and to stand before the Broodmother herself. The fires of the Molten Core burn anew, and Onyxia's Lair opens to those bold enough to challenge her.

New Raids Unlock

  • Molten Core — the elemental realm beneath Blackrock Mountain
  • Onyxia's Lair — the Broodmother's sanctum in Dustwallow Marsh

Molten Core

Deep within Blackrock Mountain, the elemental lord Ragnaros commands armies of fire. Molten giants, core hounds, and the treacherous Majordomo Executus stand between your raid and the Firelord's throne. Only the most coordinated teams will survive the molten halls.

Gear forged in these depths will define your realm's first tier of progression. Study the encounters, assign interrupts, and bring fire resistance — the Core shows no mercy.

Onyxia's Lair

The Broodmother of the Black Dragonflight awaits in her lair. Many have sought her hoard; few return to tell the tale. Her deep breath is legend — and lethal.

Deep breaths, warders, and whelp phases demand discipline. Fortune favors the bold. Good luck, heroes.
