Trial of the Crusader

Northrend Beasts, Jaraxxus, Faction Champions, Twin Val'kyr, and Anub'arak arrive in rapid succession. Heroism timers, tight enrage windows, and PvP-style coordination on the champions fight keep raids on their toes.

Farm heroic attempts for BiS trinkets before Icecrown looms.
