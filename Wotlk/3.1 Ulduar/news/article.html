Deep in the Storm Peaks, the Titan vault of Ulduar unlocks. Fourteen bosses, optional hard modes, and Yogg-Saron's sanity mechanic make this one of WoW's most celebrated raids.

Raid Unlocks

  • Ulduar — 10/25-player Titan facility with hard-mode toggles

Ulduar

Flame Leviathan's vehicle assault opens the gates. XT-002, Hodir, Thorim, and Freya each offer optional challenges for upgraded loot. The General Vezax and Yogg-Saron encounters punish greed and low sanity alike.

Algalon the Observer awaits those who activate every hard mode — a fitting reward for perfectionists.

In the face of madness, keep your wits. Ulduar demands nothing less.
