Zul'Aman

Nalorakk, Jan'alai, Akil'zon, and Hex Lord Malacrass guard the path to Zul'jin. The gauntlet run rewards mounts and chest upgrades for raids that still push speed. Even without the timer, the loot table keeps Zul'Aman relevant deep into tier 6.

Sharpen your routes, assign crowd control, and respect the bear.
