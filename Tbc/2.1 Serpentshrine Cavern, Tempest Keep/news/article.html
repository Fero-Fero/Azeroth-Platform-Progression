Outland's deadliest strongholds open simultaneously — Vashj's naga lurk beneath Coilfang Reservoir while Kael'thas Sunstrider holds Tempest Keep. Tier 5 progression splits your raid across land and sky.

New Raids Unlock

  • Serpentshrine Cavern — Lady Vashj's underwater lair
  • Tempest Keep — four wings culminating in Kael'thas

Serpentshrine Cavern

The Underbog was only the entrance. Hydross, Leotheras, and the infamous Vashj encounter demand swim speed, precise tethering, and raid-wide awareness. Striders and spore bats are unforgiving teachers.

Tempest Keep

The Blood Elf prince rebuilt his power in Netherstorm. Void Reaver, Solarian, and the weapons of the Phoenix phase test your DPS checks before the legendary Kael'thas fight — one of Burning Crusade's most cinematic finales.

Coordinate two raid nights, share loot councils wisely, and push into tier 5 sets.
